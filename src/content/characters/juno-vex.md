Juno Vex rides a bipedal scout frame named *Morrowhound*, a relic unit that should have rusted into folklore decades ago. Instead, she keeps it moving with custom joints, stolen coolant, and the sort of practical loyalty frontier mechanics reserve for impossible equipment.

Her job is equal parts rescue work and deterrence. She escorts caravans through landslides, clears radio dead zones, and reminds raiders that the border still has a pulse.

## Patrol Notes

- Maintains a network of field shelters along the pass.
- Carries analog tools because storms shred modern targeting systems.
- Treats every civilian rescue as higher priority than jurisdictional arguments.
