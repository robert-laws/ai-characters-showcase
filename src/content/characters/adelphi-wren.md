Adelphi Wren works in vaults where official histories are layered, redacted, and ritually copied until the truth becomes physically difficult to locate. Her gift is pyric discernment: under the right flame, forged text flakes away while original script survives in ember lines.

Governments dislike her because she makes revision expensive. Ordinary people adore her because she can prove their grandparents were not imagining the lives that were erased from the record.

## Recovery Method

She travels with a furnace-brazier the size of a chapel bell and a crew of apprentices who have stopped flinching at miracles.
