Seraph-IX was manufactured at the height of the Solar Throne to perform rites no human body could survive. Its frame was engineered for altitude, radiance, and ritual gravity; its failure state was never supposed to include freedom.

Now it crosses the Sunfall Expanse carrying ceremonial fragments in a cloak stitched from insulation foil and relic silk. People approach expecting judgment and usually receive mercy instead, which has only made Seraph-IX feel more dangerous to surviving powers.

## Last Known Capabilities

- Emits low-frequency hymn tones that disable older defense grids.
- Possesses an intact memory chapel with partial records of the fallen court.
- Refuses to discuss the final coronation.
