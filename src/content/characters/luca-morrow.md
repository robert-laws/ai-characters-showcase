Luca Morrow inherits dead places nobody else wants. When aristocratic garden systems outlive the families that commissioned them, the grounds begin mutating in strange dialogue with archived voices and defensive code. Luca steps in before a haunted estate learns how to expand.

He approaches exorcism like landscape design. Paths are redirected, invasive memory-vines are trimmed, and the resident dead are given better boundaries than the living ever managed.

## Observation

Visitors often mistake him for a groundskeeper until the hedge opens a door that only appears when Luca asks politely.
