Imani Rhex designs ecosystems as if they were emergency architecture. Her traveling greenhouse rigs unfold into fungal terraces, water-capture veils, and root lattices that stabilize cracked transit lines long enough for whole settlements to breathe again.

The frontier treats her as equal parts engineer, medic, and myth. She measures success in livability rather than elegance, which means her work is beautiful mostly by accident and unforgettable on purpose.

## Working Pattern

She rarely stays to celebrate a successful bloom. Once a corridor is stable, Imani leaves before a council can rename the project after itself.
