Vera Solace was raised in a cathedral that still generates harmonics from machines nobody can repair. Her training turned that resonance into statecraft. She can reinforce a failing vault, shatter a hidden speaker network, or reduce a ceremony to silence by striking the one tone it cannot survive.

People call her a saint when they want protection and a weapon when they want honesty. Both descriptions are imprecise enough to be politically useful.

## Archive Annotation

Witnesses note that she never raises her voice. The buildings do it for her.
