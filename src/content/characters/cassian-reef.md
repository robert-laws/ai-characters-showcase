Cassian Reef pilots retrofitted cargo skiffs across the Undertow Quarter, where old factories sink a little deeper each season and streets become waterways without warning. He knows which district gates still open below the tide line and which checkpoints accept silence as a valid bribe.

His clients include refugees, archivists, and exactly the kind of people respectable councils publicly denounce while privately retaining on standby.

## Route Intelligence

- Prefers quiet engines over fast ones.
- Marks safe passages with lantern colors no official chart acknowledges.
- Runs parallel supply lines during storm season when ground transport becomes ceremonial fiction.
