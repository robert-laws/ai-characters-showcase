Kael Strand serves the throne like a man born inside a thunderhead. His armor houses an antique pressure spine that lets him bend local weather just enough to tilt a duel toward inevitability. He does not summon storms so much as persuade them to arrive early.

The Cinder Court presents him as living tradition, but his presence functions more like a warning shot. When Kael appears in a diplomatic hall, everyone understands that negotiation has already narrowed.

## Combat Profile

- Favors close-range engagements where visibility can be broken on command.
- Known for ending fights before the first formal challenge is fully spoken.
- Avoids public triumph; every victory is staged to look like solemn necessity.
