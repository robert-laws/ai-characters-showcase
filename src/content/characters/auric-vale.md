Auric Vale moves through the desert rail-yards of the Glass Wastes like a coronation that forgot how to end. He buys dead war machines by the ton, restores their memory cores, and sells the stories back to ruined houses that still need a myth to salute.

His claim to royalty is technically false, politically useful, and emotionally contagious. Every city-port he visits grows a circle of people who want him to be the lost heir because the alternative is admitting the dynasty already ended.

## Field Notes

- Travels with a convoy of mirrored cargo walkers called the Lantern Court.
- Keeps relic chassis polished to ceremonial shine even when they no longer function.
- Has never been photographed the same way twice in witness reports, which has made him harder to arrest and easier to romanticize.
