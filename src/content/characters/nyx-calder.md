Nyx Calder built a reputation on routes that should not exist. During rolling grid failures, they move between tower districts using maintenance scaffolds, silent gliders, and forgotten service shafts older than the present government.

To the authorities, Nyx is a trafficking problem with excellent posture. To the districts that survive because medicine keeps appearing after curfew, they are closer to a civic utility.

## Delivery Notes

- Never repeats the same extraction path.
- Uses eclipse-black visors that strip away facial recognition signatures.
- Accepts payment in power cells, sealed names, or access codes rather than currency alone.
